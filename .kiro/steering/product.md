# Product Overview

QRC-EV is a research project applying Quantum Reservoir Computing to electric vehicle charging demand forecasting. This is the first application of QRC to the EV charging domain.

## Core Purpose

Systematically compare six quantum reservoir architectures and three classical baselines across three public EV charging datasets, with validation on IBM Quantum hardware.

## Key Innovation

- Zero prior work applies QRC to EV charging or transportation energy domains
- QRC avoids barren plateaus (no gradient optimization) — trains via simple ridge regression
- Hardware noise acts as implicit regularizer
- Novel integration of exogenous EV sales/adoption data as quantum features

## Target Outcome

Q2 2026 journal submission demonstrating QRC feasibility for EV charging forecasting, with comprehensive architecture comparison and real quantum hardware validation.

## Research Scope

This is a research project, not production software. Focus is on reproducibility, statistical rigor, and publication-quality results.
